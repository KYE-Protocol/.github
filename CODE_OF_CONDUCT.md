# Code of Conduct

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) without modification. The full text is below; the abridged operating rules follow.

## In short

- **Treat others' time as scarce.** Cite specifics. Don't make people guess what you mean.
- **Assume good faith** until evidence says otherwise. Then say something.
- **No marketing-only posts.** Share something the community can run, read, or evaluate.
- **No harassment.** Not in issues, not in discussions, not in PR review, not in DMs.
- **English-language posts** in the public spaces, please — until we have moderators in other languages.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to every public space the project owns: GitHub repositories under `KYE-Protocol/*`, the public Discussions forum, the landing page, and any community calls or events held under the project's name.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour can be reported to the maintainers via the contact paths listed in the [org profile README](https://github.com/KYE-Protocol/.github/blob/main/profile/README.md). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining consequences:

1. **Correction** — private warning, clarification of expected behaviour.
2. **Warning** — a public or private warning with consequences for continued behaviour.
3. **Temporary ban** — temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community.
4. **Permanent ban** — permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
